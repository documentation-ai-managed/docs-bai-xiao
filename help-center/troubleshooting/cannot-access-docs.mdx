description: Steps to resolve documentation access issues in bai xiao.
---

## Symptoms
- Page not loading
- "Access Denied" error

## Causes
- Not logged in
- Insufficient permissions

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Visit bai xiao and ensure you are signed in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ask your project admin for access if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Try refreshing or clearing your browser cache.
  </Step>
</Steps>
