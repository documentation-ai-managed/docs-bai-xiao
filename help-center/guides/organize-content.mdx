## Overview

Create a clear structure for your bai xiao docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add sections">
    Use the sidebar to create new sections for topics, guides, or admin info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add pages">
    In each section, click "Add Page" and give it a clear title.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag and arrange">
    Reorder sections and pages by dragging them in the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>
