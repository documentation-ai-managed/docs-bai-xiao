## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for account verification
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- No prior account on Bai Xiao (sign up is free)

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register and log in to Bai Xiao.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the [Bai Xiao dashboard](https://app.bai-xiao.com) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click the `Sign Up` button. Fill in your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Bai Xiao. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard and enter your credentials. You now access your account dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication after login for added security.
</Callout>

## Create a New Project Space

Project spaces organize your documentation. Create one to group related docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click `New Space` in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and choose a description. Select `Public` or `Private` visibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Set your brand color to `#3B82F6` and add an optional logo. Click `Create Space`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Document

Populate your space with content using MDX.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    Inside your space, click `New Document`. Name it `Introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Frontmatter" icon="code">
    Start with YAML frontmatter for metadata:

````markdown
---
title: My First Document
description: Welcome to Bai Xiao documentation.
---
```
````

  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Content" icon="edit">
    Add headings and text:

    ```mdx
    ## Overview

    This is your first doc in Bai Xiao.
    ```

    Preview changes in real-time by clicking the eye icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload">
    Click `Publish` to make it live. Share the URL with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
